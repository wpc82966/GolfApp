About Bay Hill
Bay Hill Club & Lodge
9000 Bay Hill Blvd, Orlando, FL 32819

Home of the Arnold Palmer Invitational, Bay Hill is one of Florida's most prestigious private clubs. Designed by Dick Wilson and renovated by Arnold Palmer himself, the course is known for its demanding par-4 finishing hole playing over water — one of the most iconic closing holes in professional golf.
